Project Overview

Application Summary

This website will be a portfolio site to showcase Maya's artwork and provide information about her services. The site will feature a gallery of her work, details about the types of art she creates, and a contact form for potential clients to reach out.

  • Working title: Maya Hutaglung's Portfolio
  • Intended users: Maya, Job interviewers, friends and family.
  • Content overview: Key sections include an art gallery with an interactive display. An about me page. A contact page. Specific portfolios based on different types of art. A page to demonstrate the previous work she has done for clients what she has been featured in.
